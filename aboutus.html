About Ridex

Ridex, founded by Mr. Akshay Devadiga, is a transportation company that provides a platform for users to book both taxi and bike services. Similar to popular ride-sharing platforms, Ridex offers convenient and on-demand transportation solutions. Users can access the services through a mobile app, allowing them to book rides efficiently and reliably. The company aims to provide a seamless and reliable transportation experience, catering to both taxi and bike commuters in its operational areas.
